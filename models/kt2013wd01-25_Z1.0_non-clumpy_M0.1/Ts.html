Surface Temperature

pdrtpy ModelSet: wk2006

Surface Temperature emitted from the surface of a PDR as a function of the cloud density n and the FUV flux incident on the cloud G0 .     The models in this ModelSet were created with the KOSMA-tau PDR code. More details are in the FITS headers. The FITS file is available for download.
