Ratio of CO (J=2-1) / CO (J=1-0) line intensities

pdrtpy ModelSet: wk2006

Ratio of CO (J=2-1) / CO (J=1-0) line intensities emitted from the surface of a PDR as a function of the cloud density \(n\) and the FUV flux incident on the cloud \(G_0\) .     Models of Kaufman et al. 1999 are used with the standard model parameter set. The FITS file is available for download.
